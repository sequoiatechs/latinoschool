About Latino School

Learn, Live and Love.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide a tailored, immersive education experience in some of the richest cultural environments available in Latin America.

OUR VISION

Our students will use their experience here to advance their careers, enhance their personal lives, and positively impact their communities.

VALUES

Integrity, Critical Analysis, Social Responsibility, Promotion of Diversity, Service and Effective Communication.

.

WHO WE ARE

We are a family owned, non-profit institution founded in 1989 by Carlos del Corral and Rosita Ortiz de del Corral.

We have a long tradition of providing top-quality language instruction and wonderful homestay experiences to students of all ages from many different countries throughout the world.

In addition to language immersion, we also offer unique opportunities for studying Business, Entrepreneurial Development, Spanish as a Second Language, and general Liberal Arts Studies including coursework in History, Indigenous Culture, Medical Vocabulary and more.

You have the option to study for university credit or for personal enrichment. In addition, you can give back to the local community through volunteering or an internship.

Our courses accommodate the complete Beginner through Advanced levels, operating year-round, with open enrollment dates.
Our excellent testing program allows student placement at their exact level, and weekly evaluations allow students to move at their own pace.
They are continually challenged, but not frustrated by being slightly ahead or slightly behind their classmates.
