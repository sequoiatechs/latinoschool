Prices

All prices are in US dollars. Weekly Spanish Program includes 5 days of classes (from Monday to Friday) and 7 days accommodation with a host family or at the school residence. Accommodation starts on Sunday before you start classes and ends on Saturday after you finish classes. Board (at the host family only): 2 meals per day (breakfast and dinner in Ecuador, breakfast and lunch in Peru and Bolivia), laundry service once a week. Flavors of the Andes: 3 meals per day. At the student residence there are no meals or laundry included, however there's a shared kitchen to prepare their own meals. Also included in the weekly rate: placement test, city tour, orientation session and course certificate. Mail service, money exchange, wireless access to internet, luggage storage. Security boxes at extra charge. Course starting dates: Every Monday all year round. (Please check holidays in each country). Available extra services: see below.
