Weekly Spanish Program includes 5 days of classes (from Monday to Friday) and 7 days accommodation with a host family or at the school residence.

Accommodation starts on Sunday before you start classes and ends on Saturday after you finish classes.

Board (at the host family only): 2 meals per day (breakfast and dinner in Ecuador, breakfast and lunch in Peru and Bolivia), laundry service once a week. Flavors of the Andes: 3 meals per day.

At the student residence there are no meals or laundry included, however there's a shared kitchen to prepare your own meals.

Also included in the weekly rate: placement test, city tour, orientation session and course certificate.

Mail service, money exchange, wireless access to internet - wifi, luggage storage. Security boxes at extra charge.

Course starting dates: Every Monday all year round. (Please check holidays in each country).

Our courses accommodate the complete Beginner through Advanced levels, operating year-round, with open enrollment dates.
Our excellent testing program allows student placement at their exact level, and weekly evaluations allow students to move at their own pace.
They are continually challenged, but not frustrated by being slightly ahead or slightly behind their classmates.
