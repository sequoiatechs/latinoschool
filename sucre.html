PREMIUM LOCATION IN SUCRE


The Academia Latinoamericana in Bolivia is located in the heart of Sucre, just one block away from the city center within close proximity to virtually everything of interest within the city.

Classes are in the mornings, which leave the afternoons free for cultural activities, or half day excursions.

Classes are held in a 2 story building with (11) classrooms, kitchen, a lounge (for videos, etc.) 2 terraces, one indoor patio and much more.

Sucre is the beautiful colonial judicial capital located in the Andean highlands of Bolivia. Known as the "White City of the Americas" due to its numerous colonial buildings, all of which are painted in white and topped with shiny red tile roofs.

Set in a valley surrounded by low mountains, Sucre is small (pop. 150,000) so that it is very easy to explore on foot.


ACCOMMODATIONS IN SUCRE


We want to remind you of the special hospitality and warmth of a loving South American family. Truly this is impossible to describe until you have experienced it!

You will leave with a better knowledge of the Spanish language, but just as importantly, as a result of the experience of living with a host family, with a clearer understanding of the people and the rich cultural heritage of the country.

You can decide to take classes only or also pick the option to stay at our student residence hall located right next to the Academia campus, both options are excellent for our students.


FIRST DAY OF CLASSES


Academia Latinoamericana takes care of you from the very first moment you step off the plane: your host family will pick you up from the airport and take you to your home away from home. They make sure you have a great start into this adventure and get settled well into our culture.

On the first day of classes, your host family will take you the school and show you the way and where to take buses from.

All new students are given an official welcome to Academia Latinoamericana by the director of the Academia.

After a written and oral placement test you depart on a city tour of Sucre together with all new students.

A representative of the school will explain you how to get around the city, how to use public transport, where to find important places and tell you about the history of Sucre.

In the afternoon all new students participate in an orientation session, in which we tell you important rules regarding classes, schedules, host families, cultural activities and weekend trips.
