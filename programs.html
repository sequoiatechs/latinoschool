INTENSIVE PROGRAM

Our traditional Spanish program in Ecuador, Peru or Bolivia has 5 student maximum per class, 20‐hour per week group course, lots of conversation and get to know people from all over the world! In the afternoon session, you’ll have all the time to explore the beauty of countries and interacting with the local people using your newly acquired Spanish skills It is an excellent program for those who wish to quickly develop a working proficiency in Spanish.

  • Length of time: 1 to 12 weeks, 4 hours a day.

  • # of students per class: 5 students per class.

  • Advantages: Intensive classes with lots of interaction with other students

  • # of hours: 1 to 12 weeks, 4 hours a day.

  • Price: $350 per person per week including classes and host family accommodation with two meals per day

SUPER INTENSIVE PROGRAM

Semi personalized Spanish Lessons having small group lessons in the morning and private classes in the afternoon to reinforce your morning class or develop only your oral skills, allowing you to experience Spanish from all angles, simply fantastic! You can do this course in any of our campuses in Quito-Ecuador, Cusco-Peru or Sucre-Bolivia

  • Length of time: 1 to 12 weeks, 6 hours a day

  • # of students per class: 5 students per class in the morning, and 1 student with 1 teacher in the afternoon.

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Total of 30 hours per week

  • Price: $500 per person per week including classes and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

ACTIVA PROGRAM

Our intensive program offers regular courses in the morning as well as guided activities in the afternoon. If you have specific interests we can often arrange special topics courses. All transportation costs and entrance fees are included.

  • Length of time: 1 to 4 weeks, 7 hours a day

  • # of students per class: 5 students per class

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Total of 35 hours per week

  • Price: $660 per person per week including classes, activities with transportation, entrances, tickets and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

INDIVIDUAL PROGRAM

Our One-on-One program is tailored to your strengths and weaknesses so that you can learn at your own pace on your schedule. This program is ideal for professionals seeking to expand field-specific vocabulary, as well as travelers with limited schedules.

  • Length of time: 1 to 12 weeks

  • # of students per class: Individual classes

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Total of 20 or 30 hours per week

  • Price: $470 or $620 per person per week including classes and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

SEMI PRIVATE PROGRAM (2/1)

Semi personalized Spanish Lessons with just you and your wife and the teacher in Quito-Ecuador, Cusco-Peru or Sucre-Bolivia

  • Length of time: 1 to 12 weeks, 4 hours a day

  • # of students per class: 2 students with 1 teacher

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Total of 20 hours per week

  • Price: $410 per person per week including classes and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

TEACHER METHODOLOGY PROGRAM

A series of seminars on the teaching of culture, literature, grammar, conversation and composition. Students will collect authentic materials for the classroom and will use them to devise spiraled lesson plans for their own classes in the United States. Term paper and weekly seminar with program director required. Lab experience includes conversational interchange with a native and analyses of journals.

  • Length of time: 2 weeks

  • # of students per class: 12 students per class

  • Advantages: Super dynamic course

  • # of hours: Total of 40 hours per week

  • Price: $1,220 per person for the two week program including classes, activities with transportation, entrances, tickets and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

SPANISH CLASSES ONLINE

Online Spanish lessons give you the freedom and flexibility to learn at your own pace and on your own time. Whether you are an advanced speaker wanting to perfect your skills or you are just beginning to learn the language, we will tailor your lessons to ensure that you are receiving the individualized attention you deserve.

  • Length of time: 1 to 16 weeks

  • # of students per class: 1 student with 1 teacher

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Optional

  • Price: $15 dollars per hour

SENIOR PROGRAM

Our program for Senior Citizens utilizes in-class instruction and experiential learning. You will participate in educational excursions in and around the city. Additionally, you can partake in our volunteer programs, where you will serve some of the poorest communities in our area.

  • Length of time: 1 to 4 weeks, 7 hours a day

  • # of students per class: 1 student with 1 teacher

  • Advantages: Personalized curriculum

  • # of hours: Total of 35 hours per week

  • Price: $780 per person per week including classes, activities with transportation, entrances, tickets and host family accommodation with two meals per day.

SIELE & DELE PREPARATION COURSE

We've got what you need to achieve results in the world’s #1 Spanish level test, SIELE & DELE. Our special Spanish courses can be customized for your exam level and your needs

Bring a group

The Academia has been creating itineraries and hosting groups of up to 45 students for over 28 years. Planning a group trip with the Academia provides peace of mind knowing that you are bringing your students to one of the most well respected language schools in South America! Our over 23 years of language immersion experience allow us to plan the trip most appropriate for your group based on your established parameters while maximizing your budget. We take care of all the pre-trip planning to ensure a smooth running itinerary during your South American Oddissey experience. If you have 5 or more participants, please contact Dr. Diego del Corral to diego@latinoschools.com to begin planning your Spanish immersion adventure!
