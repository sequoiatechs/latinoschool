Internships

INTERNSHIPS & VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

The Academia provides its students with unique opportunities to combine Spanish language study with Internship or volunteer work in one of the many beautiful national parks or reserves, a social service organization, a women's or senior citizen's organization, NGOs, educational institutions, health care projects and more.

Get to know this beautiful country as an insider while making a difference!

Our courses accommodate the complete Beginner through Advanced levels, operating year-round, with open enrollment dates.
