PREMIUM LOCATION IN QUITO


The city of Quito is located in a beautiful valley surrounded by the snow capped peaks of the Andes, it is a Franciscan city and surprisingly friendlier and safer feeling than other large Latin American cities. Furthermore, it has kept its colonial charm in spite of growing to the size of two million. The temperature is determinate by the altitude and the averages 60-70 during the day and 50 during the night.

The Academia Latinoamericana in QUITO, is set in the residential area of La Carolina, next to La Carolina park (great place for students since they can practice sports such us soccer, basket, volley, jogging, aerobics, roller skating, etc and about 10.000 quitenos practice sports specially Saturday and Sunday), it is also close to the Metropolitan park, the biggest green area in Quito, which is a great place to go in order to do some jogging, running, mountain biking and have a fantastic view of the valley of Quito.

The school is located is a small street called Noruega, which is a residential area, full of good restaurants, galleries, five shopping centers, many hotels around the school, several internet cafes, and much more!

The Academia has a colonial design, with interior patio, 21 classrooms, video room with amplification equipment, library, teachers room, wireless internet service, a terrace with a fantastic view of the Pichincha volcano (active volcano with close to 14.000 feet) access and bathroom for wheelchair users and elevator; participants are half from Europe, half from the United States. The majority are professionals and university students.


I WANT TO STAY IN QUITO


We want to remind you of the special hospitality and warmth of a loving South American family. Truly this is impossible to describe until you have experienced it!

You will leave with a better knowledge of the Spanish language, but just as importantly, as a result of the experience of living with a host family, with a clearer understanding of the people and the rich cultural heritage of the country.

You can decide to take classes only or also pick the option to stay at our student residence hall located right next to the Academia campus, both options are excellent for our students.


FIRST DAY OF CLASSES


Academia Latinoamericana takes care of you from the very first moment you step off the plane: your host family will pick you up from the airport and take you to your home away from home.

They make sure you have a great start into this adventure and get settled well into our culture.

On the first day of classes, your host family will take you the school and show you the way and where to take buses from. All new students are given an official welcome to Academia Latinoamericana by the director of the Academia.

After a written and oral placement test you depart on a city tour of Quito together with all new students.

A representative of the school will explain you how to get around the city, how to use public transport, where to find important places and tell you about the history of Quito.

In the afternoon all new students participate in an orientation session, in which we tell you important rules regarding classes, schedules, host families, cultural activities and weekend trips.

Important topics such as security in Ecuador, do’s and don’ts and general things you need to be aware of during your stay are covered as well, so you can feel completely home and entirely enjoy your stay in our country.


Your weekday in Ecuador


8:30am: Welcome and meet your new friends and teachers at Academia Latinoamericana.

8:30-10:30am: Classes and lectures – Practice grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, written expression.

10:30-11am: Pause – Enjoy the international atmosphere at the school and socialize with your new friends from anywhere in the world.

11am-1pm: Classes and lectures – Practice oral comprehension and expression in discussions, rule plays, games, etc.

1-2pm: Free time for lunch – Relax with your friends and get your lunch at one of the many local restaurants, take away places or parks near the school.

2-5pm: Optional activities - Explore the city and its surroundings on one of the many cultural excursions and visit local sights, museums or markets and learn about our countries’ captivating history - or simply hang out with your new friends.

7pm: Dinner with your host family – Practice your Spanish in a relaxed, homely atmosphere and get to know Ecuadorian culture first-hand from conversations with the natives.
