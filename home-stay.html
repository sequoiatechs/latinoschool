Host Family

The host family takes care of our students and is a wonderful support network.

Most of our families are within walking distance to the Academia in all our locations.

They provide two meals a day, airport pick up, and do your laundry as well.

Some families have more than one bedroom for couples and parents with children.

All the families are well screened and truly add to the learning environment.

The best part of living with a family is the day-to-day interactions and exchanges with them.

If you wish other family members or friends to live with you during your stay, every effort will be made to accommodate your request.

ACADEMIA STUDENT RESIDENCE IN QUITO


Located next door of Academia Latinoamericana. Shared and single room available. Lounge with small tv-radio, stoked kitchen & fireplace available for students.

Students can bring their own laptop and unlimited access to internet for free.

Easy access to public transportation, lots of restaurants and a very good one just across the street from the Academia and the student residence.

We are also very close to the Carolina park only walk distance to the main campus, this is a large recreational park where Quito goes to relax outdoors. On weekends the park is buzzing with soccer matches, volleyball games, skateboarding, outdoor aerobic classes, paddleboats, family games and picnics. A botanical-orchid garden and dinosaur museum have also been recently added to the park.

Several of Quito's major shopping centers surround La Carolina including the Quicentro, Megamaxi, El Jardin, el Caracol and Inaquito. These shopping malls offer a variety of both local and imported goods as well as supermarkets. Going east we can also find the Metropolitan park, the biggest green area in Quito, which is a great place to go in order to do some jogging, running, mountain biking and have a fantastic view of the valley of Quito.

ACADEMIA STUDENT RESIDENCE IN CUSCO


Academia student residence is located in the principal residential and sfetiest district of Cusco called Mariscal Gamarra located only 15 minutes to the Academia campus and Cusco historic down town.

The house that has an excellent location for all amenities; a few minutes away from the cultural and commercial areas like supermarkets la Canasta and Orion; a half block is Avenida de la Cultura, one of Cusco’s main avenue, and within the area you can find the Real Plaza Mall where you can find many restaurants, shops, cafeterias as Café Valdez, Starbucks, banks, pubs, cinemas, souvenirs and many other things.

The priority of the Academia student house consists in providing flexible and adapted accommodation to the requirements of the students who come to study at the Academia or just travelling around the area. This flexibility turns the house into a good option for long or short stays, giving them a personalized attention.

The student house represents the perfect balance between the private and social life. It’s an innovative concept thought for young international public of open minded, ready for living and for sharing a fresh, urban way of life and for having new friends where you will be able to share with them cooking activities, trekings, travels, pubs, discos, restaurants and much more.

Book right now your room and assure a place with us, it will be your best choice to live through an unforgettable semester in Peru.

ACADEMIA STUDENT RESIDENCE IN SUCRE

Room & Services

We have 18 comfortable and wide rooms, with 12 and one half bathroom.

Wide facilities, large living room with cable tv, dvd, and dinning room.

Every bathroom has shower and warm water 24 hours a day.

Lovely wide terrace with tables, parasols and chairs.

Well proportioned rooms with writing desks, chairs, closets, wardrobe, and beside table.

One bathroom every two rooms in double rooms and private rooms with bathroom.

Kitchen with full catering equipment, with three fridges, microwave, stove, pots, glasses, etc.

All bed linen is provided (sheets, blankets, comforters, and pillows).

Our courses accommodate the complete Beginner through Advanced levels, operating year-round, with open enrollment dates.
Our excellent testing program allows student placement at their exact level, and weekly evaluations allow students to move at their own pace.
They are continually challenged, but not frustrated by being slightly ahead or slightly behind their classmates.
