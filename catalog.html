UNIVERSITY CREDIT & COURSE CATALOG

Course Catalog

Earn 3 credits in only 3 weeks of classes!

Students travelling to Ecuador, Peru or Bolivia may earn university transfer credits while studying at one or all three of our fantastic locations in the Andean region.

Undergraduate & graduate credit is available at all levels, from beginner Spanish to upper-level courses, and is fully transferable to other accredited universities.

Many USA/CANADIAN teachers receive continuing education credits with their school district through our program. Academia Latinoamericana’s overseas programs have been nationally recognized for their excellence.

If student wishes to come during the summer the minimum lenght of time is three (3) weeks for one course (3 credits) or if a student comes for a semester (can do a 12 or 17 weeks program with 15 credits. Student must select 5 courses).

NEED UNIVERSITY CREDIT?

Most students receive credit directly with their matriculating university. Or, student can apply for credit for Spanish & Humanities courses through New Mexico State University (NMSU) or Brookhaven College.

Please make all arrangements directly through New Mexico State University prior to beginning the program.
